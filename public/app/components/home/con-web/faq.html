Frequently Asked Questions

Q.What is your refund policy?

A. A full refund of your deposit will be issued as long so long as the request is made before the driver has been dispatched to your job. Once the driver has been dispatched your deposit will no longer be refundable. To make a cancellation and retrieve a full refund please send us an email, with the subject “job cancelation” to hello@thevanclub.io. In the email you must list the job ID and the date of the move. Once this is done we will reply to you within 24 hours with a job cancellation notification and the refund will be processed within 48 hrs and will reach your account within 7 days.

Q.What happens if our driver doesn’t turn up?

A. Once you have made a booking we will allocate your job to one of our drivers. Once your job has been allocated you will be sent a text message with the contact details for your driver. This will allow you to contact you driver directly. If you and the driver reach an agreement on timings which is different to the specified details on the booking we will not interfere and you will not be liable for any refund. If for any reason you cannot get in touch with your driver and you are concerned that he may not be able to attend your booking you may contact us so we can either get in touch with your driver, or reallocate to another should there be a reason your driver cannot attend. If we still cannot complete your delivery according to your satisfaction we will issue you a refund of your deposit.

Q.My payment is not working?

A. Payments may not work for the following reasons. 1. Your long card number on the front of the card is incorrect 2. Your Expiry date is not correct 3. The 3 digits on the back of the card is incorrect 4. You have insufficient funds in your account. If none of the above reasons are correct then please call us so that we can look into it.

Q.I have reached the cubic feet limit?

A. Our website calculates the cubic feet of all the things you want to be delivered. However if this cubic feet extends beyond 600 cubic we ask you to give us a call as this is a bigger job task than usual and we want to make sure we estimate the job properly to ensure we allocate the most appropriate type of van.

Q.How do I get In contact with my driver?

A. Once you have processed a booking, we will allocate a driver that is best suited to complete your job. Once this is done you will get an automated text message that will include the contact number for your driver. You can use this to contact the driver for any updated information and to get help with anything you need.

Q.How do I know the right sized van is coming?

A. Our website calculates an approximate cubic feet volume based on your item input and suggests a van size based on the rate this qualifies for. The driver also receives a list of your items to ensure consistency when carrying out your job. If for any reason you feel like the van that we are allocating is not the one you need please give us a call to manually change the allocated vehicle type.

Q.I need extra drop offs?

A. Our app currently only supports single dropoffs, you will need to create a fresh booking if you would like a second collection to be added. If you request the driver to make extra stops he/she will not be able to do this.

Q.I don’t want to pay the deposit by card can I pay by it all by cash?

A. No. Unfortunately we cannot allocate any bookings without a deposit and formal booking beforehand.

Q.What happens if I want to add more items to my delivery after i have booked?

A. If after you have completed your booking you realise you want to add extra items to your delivery send us an email or give us a call to add these. You may be charged extra by our customer service team which will then be charged to your card on file.

Q.What is an extra helper?

A. If you select an extra driver your driver will bring an extra worker with him to help with the work. You should select this service if you have items that you think will be too big for one man to load and unload.

Q.I don’t have a confirmed delivery date?

A. If you do not currently have a firm delivery date please come back to book once you do have one.

Q.I am A driver how do I start to do deliveries for The Van Club?

A. If you would like to sign up to work as a provider for The Van Club then please click here and fill out the form. After we have vetted your application we will then contact you for the next steps to set you up with your driver app.
